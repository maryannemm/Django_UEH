Uzury Empowerment Hub

This is a community based organization that focuses on harnessing community effort and resources to support empowerment by fostering a transformative culture. Our objective it to create an environment where women, girls, and vulnerable populations are free to achieve their full potential.

In a world that is constantly changing, there is a need to empower women and girls with skills to ensure they are ready and able to take full advantage of these opportunities.

MISSION:

Supporting women, girls, and vulnerable populations to achieve their overall well-being and reduce their vulnerability to social injustices and mental health issues.

What do we want to achieve?

Through the Women Leadership Committee, we hope to work together to create programs and interventions that are relevant, effective, and tailor made to support women and girls by providing them with knowledge, skills, and services that help in responding to and preventing gender injustices.

This is done through:

  1. Advocacy and lobbying
  2. Social mobilization
  3. Capacity building
  4. Partnerships and collaborations
  5. Youth led research
  6. Mentorship
